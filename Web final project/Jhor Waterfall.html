Are you looking for a quick escape this summer? If yes then Jhor Waterfall is the destination. Jhor Waterfall is a natural waterfall that lies in Tokha, Kathmandu. Moreover, the waterfall is on the northern side of the Kathmandu Valley. The Waterfall is surrounded by beautiful monuments of Hindu religions. This includes a famous temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is Barundeshor Mahadev Temple which lies alongside the Tokha Jhor Waterfall. Meanwhile, the temple remains crowded during the Shrawn month of the Nepali Calendar.

The beautiful Jhor Waterfall originates from Shivapuri Jungle. Moreover, the presence of Barundeshor Mahadev Temple beside the waterfall adds beauty to the place. The waterfall lies on the way from Samakhusi to Trisuli Bazaar of Nuwakot. The flow of water at the waterfall is smaller in the winter seasons compared to that of the summer seasons. As the waterfall lies a short distance from the Ring Road of Kathmandu Valley, this waterfall attracts most of the internal tourists during the summer seasons.

Located at the beautiful Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park, Jhor waterfall is 10 km away from the Samakhusi Chowk at Ring Road of Kathmandu Valley. Moreover, religious monuments and temples near the waterfall attract many tourists to this area. This is a renowned waterfall along Sundarijal Waterfall for visitors near Kathmandu Valley who want to experience Waterfall. Meanwhile, the natural spring water helps travelers beat the summer’s heat. Similarly, this place is a popular destination for hiking as well. This place is a beautiful and serene destination to escape from the hustling life of Kathmandu City.

Why is Jhor Waterfall Popular?

Jhor Waterfall famous by other names Baudeshwor Jharana or Baudeshwor Waterfall. Due to the popularity of this place, the local government also has given priority to the development of this place. Likewise, this place is well maintained and looked after by Tokha Municipality. This beautiful waterfall lies on the border between Kathmandu and Nuwakot Districts. The Tokha Jhor is easily accessible from Kathmandu. Meanwhile, visitors can reach the destination by hiking, cycling, riding, or on a public bus. The scenery of the beautiful waterfall after reaching the place erases all the tiredness of reaching there.

Reaching the waterfall offers you a beautiful landscape view of Kathmandu Valley. Moreover, you can have mesmerizing views of panoramic mountains on a clear day from there as well. This place not only offers a waterfall but has a holy temple of Lord Shiva as well. Visiting this place offers you to take a shower in the cold natural waterfall coming out from Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park. Also, you get an opportunity to offer prayers to Lord Shiva and seek blessings from him. The easy accessibility, full of natural beauty, and the presence of a holy place, these all things make the place popular.

History of Barundeshor Mahadev Temple

The history of this place is quite interesting. Bhashmeshwar mediated and prayed to Lord Shiva (Mahadev) in ancient times. Lord Mahadev was happy and impressed by the prayer and meditation of Bhashmeshwar. Meanwhile, Lord Mahadev gave power to Bhashmeshwar. The boon of Bhashmeshwar was that anything Bhashmeshwar touches would be extinct or converted to ash (Bhasma). After Bhashmeshwar got that power, he tried to use the power to Lord Shiva, Mahadev. To save himself, Lord Mahadev came to the cave of the Jhor and lived there inside the cave.

The Second Jhor Waterfall

The main Jhor Waterfall or Jhor Jharana lies alongside the Barundeshor Mahadev Temple. Just below the main Jhor Waterfall, there lies another waterfall. Meanwhile, the second water originates from the main waterfall. The waterfall below the main waterfall is smaller compared to that of the main waterfall. You need to take the concrete way below to reach the second waterfall. Likewise, it may take around 2-3 minutes to reach the second waterfall below.

FAQS

How far is Jhor Waterfall from KTM?

Jhor Waterfall lies approximately 10 kilometers north of Samakhusi Chowk, Kathmandu.

Is it safe to visit Jhor Waterfall?

Yes, it is safe to visit Jhor Waterfall. However, it would be best if you took some safety precautions. Visitors need to wear proper footwear and avoid climbing or jumping on the rocks around the waterfall.

How long does it take to hike to the Jhor Waterfall?

It takes about 2-3 hours to hike from Samakhusi Chowk to the Waterfall.

Are there any nearby accommodations for visitors to stay overnight?

Yes, there are several guesthouses, resorts, and hotels in nearby areas where you can stay overnight.
