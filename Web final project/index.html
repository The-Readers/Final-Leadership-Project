Kalinchowk is a popular tourist destination located in the Dolakha district of the eastern hilly region of Nepal. The name comes from two words, Kali and Chowk. The first word “Kali” is a “Hindu goddess”, and the second word, "Chowk”, means “a flat area”. Thus, Kalinchowk means a flat area where the Hindu goddess Kali is worshipped.
